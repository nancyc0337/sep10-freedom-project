Music

Music is an arrangement of sounds that expresses ideas/emotions/messages in rhythm/melody/harmony/etc.

Ever since the early humans, music has been around for a long time.

They used banged rocks to make tools.

When they scrape rhythms and clapped their hands, they made music.

There are many different ways of making music now, but can we use technology in music?

This website will look into the technologies of both in the past and future ones.
